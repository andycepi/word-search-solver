About Crossword Solver

This is a simple crossword solving application written in C#. It was written to help solve complex crossword puzzles and supports many different languages, including English, Spanish, Italian, and more.

Features

-Reads input from a text document, with rows of letters separated by new lines -Takes words to look for from an array -Shows solutions to words by highlighting them
